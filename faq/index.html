How long does account creation take?
Account creation can take as long as 2 days. If your account has not been created in more than 2 days then please open a support ticket.
How do I add my domain?
To add your domain you will have to update your nameservers or DNS records. Our nameservers are ns1.theendlessweb.com and ns2.theendlessweb.com. Alternatively, you can use A records and point them to our IP address 149.56.38.19 or CNAME records and point them at our domain endl.site.

Note: Using A and CNAME records will not let you edit DNS records in cPanel.
How do I setup HTTPS/SSL?
Go to SSL/TLS Status under Security in cPanel and click Run AutoSSL. It might take some time for your SSL certificate to arrive at your domain.
How to redirect to HTTPS?
To automatically redirect your website to go to HTTPS you have to make a file named .htaccess in the directory your domain is located in and put the following inside it:

RewriteEngine On
RewriteCond %{HTTPS} !=on
RewriteRule ^/?(.*) https://%{SERVER_NAME}/$1 [R,L]
